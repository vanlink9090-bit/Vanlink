What VanLink is, in plain terms

VanLink is an Android app that connects school van/bus drivers directly with the parents of the students they carry — so both sides always know who they're dealing with and where the ride stands, without a single group chat or missed call.

🎯

The problem it solves

Parents waiting for a school van usually rely on informal phone numbers and word-of-mouth groups. VanLink replaces that with one verified, private connection per family.

👥

Who uses it

Two account types only: Drivers, who manage a route of students, and Parents/Guardians, who link to their child's driver. There is no separate school-admin or public account type.

💳

Cost to use

VanLink is free for drivers and parents to download and use. The app does not currently process any in-app payments, subscriptions, or purchases.

Stop 01 — How it works

From setup to your child's seat, in four steps

1. Driver sets up

A driver creates an account with their photo, license, vehicle and school details, and gets a unique 6-character code and QR code to share.

2. Parent links up

A parent adds their child's details, then enters the driver's code (or scans the QR) to connect — this is a one-time link per family.

3. Driver confirms the student

The driver sees the new family in their Pending list, fills in pickup/drop details, and confirms — this activates chat for that family.

4. The trip runs

Driver and parent chat directly, the driver can share live location for the trip, and either side can reach emergency numbers in one tap.

Frequently asked questions

The most common questions from drivers and parents.

How does a parent link to their driver?

Open Search School, find your child's school, and enter the driver's 6-character code — or scan the QR code on the driver's profile. Once linked, the driver's name, photo and vehicle appear on your home screen right away.

Where does a driver find their code?

On the Profile tab, under "Driver Code & QR." Share it with a parent once — it's a permanent link between that family and that driver.

What happens after a parent links up?

The driver sees the new family in their Pending Students list, fills in pickup and drop-off details, and confirms. Chat activates for that family the moment they confirm.

How does live location sharing work?

A driver can turn location sharing on for a trip from their app. While it's on, their linked parent(s) can see the van's live position. Turning it off stops sharing immediately, and VanLink doesn't keep a location history.

Does VanLink work without an internet connection?

You can view your previously loaded student list and recent chat messages offline. Sending messages, linking accounts, live location, and notifications all need an internet connection.

What does the Emergency button actually do?

It opens your phone's own dialer with the Ambulance, Police, or school number ready to call — VanLink doesn't place the call itself or monitor it.

Does VanLink charge drivers, parents, or schools?

No. VanLink is free to download and use for both driver and parent accounts, with no in-app payments at this time.
