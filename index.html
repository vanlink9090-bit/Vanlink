One app, built for every school van route

Every day, parents wait at the gate not knowing exactly when the van will arrive, and drivers juggle a dozen phone calls just to confirm a stop. Goschol was built to close that gap — a single, purpose-built line between the person driving, the person waiting, and the school that's responsible for both. No more scattered WhatsApp groups, no more unread messages, no more guessing.

For the driver

Everything about the route, in one place

  • Set up once with photo, license, vehicle and school details
  • Reorder your student list by dragging stops into route order
  • Confirm new families the moment they link with your code
  • Reply fast with quick messages while you're on the road
  • Reach ambulance, police, or the school in one tap
For the parent

A direct line to your child's driver

  • Add your child's details in a two-minute setup
  • Link to your driver with a single 6-character code
  • See the driver's photo, vehicle and school on one card
  • Call or message the driver without hunting for a number
  • Read messages offline if your connection drops
For the school

Oversight without the extra workload

  • Approve drivers and issue school-linked driver codes
  • See every registered van and driver on one fleet map
  • Send announcements straight to drivers or all parents
  • Keep a record of who's linked to which driver, always
  • No new hardware or SIM cards — works on any Android phone

Getting started takes minutes: a school signs up and approves its drivers, each driver gets a unique code, and parents link to their child's driver with that code — that's it. No GPS trackers to install, no separate SIM, no training required. It works on the Android phone everyone already carries, in weak-signal areas thanks to offline caching, and it's built specifically for the daily school run rather than general-purpose fleet tracking.

Stop 03 — What sets us apart

Not just tracking — built for what actually happens on the ground

These are the details most van-tracking apps skip, and the reason schools and parents stick with Goschol.

🔔
Signature feature

Arrival Alarm — impossible to miss

When a driver types something like "waiting outside" or "I'm here," Goschol doesn't just send a quiet notification — it sets off a loud, looping alarm and vibration on the parent's phone. It rides on the phone's actual alarm audio channel, so it plays even if the ringer is silenced, and it keeps going until the parent taps "Stop Alarm." A pickup should never be missed because a phone was buried in a bag.

🗺️

Floating navigation overlay

A small draggable button sits on top of Google Maps mid-route, showing "Student Name (2/5)" with arrows to jump between stops — drivers never have to leave the Maps app.

📱

Home-screen widget

A "Share Live Location" toggle plus three preset announcement buttons, usable straight from the launcher without opening the app.

🔄

Drag-to-reorder route

Drivers arrange their student pickup list by dragging stops into the actual order they drive them.

📅

Server-locked attendance

Parents mark a child absent ahead of time, but the edit cutoff is enforced against the school's server clock — not the phone's — closing the usual "change your device time" loophole.

💳

Human-verified payments

Subscribe via UPI, submit the transaction ID and screenshot, and a real admin reviews and approves it — not a black-box auto-charge.

🔍

Find your school in-app

Search and select your school directly, instead of needing a code from the driver to get started.
