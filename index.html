Know your child's ride, from the front gate to the school gate.

VanLink connects school van drivers and parents in one simple app — driver details, direct chat, and one-tap emergency calling, without the group-chat chaos.

Get VanLink See it in action

One app, two seats — driver and parent

VanLink replaces scattered phone calls and unread messages with a single, purpose-built line between the person driving and the person waiting.

For the driver

Everything about the route, in one place

  • Set up once with photo, license, vehicle and school details
  • Reorder your student list by dragging stops into route order
  • Confirm new families the moment they link with your code
  • Reply fast with quick messages while you're on the road
  • Reach ambulance, police, or the school in one tap
For the parent

A direct line to your child's driver

  • Add your child's details in a two-minute setup
  • Link to your driver with a single 6-character code
  • See the driver's photo, vehicle and school on one card
  • Call or message the driver without hunting for a number
  • Read messages offline if your connection drops
Stop 01 — Linking up
