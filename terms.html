01 Acceptance of terms

By creating an account or using VanLink ("the App"), you agree to these Terms & Conditions and our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App. These terms apply equally to Driver and Parent accounts.

02 Who can use VanLink

VanLink is intended for use by school transport drivers and by the parents or legal guardians of students using that transport. Account holders must be at least 18 years old. Driver accounts must be created by an authorized driver or transport operator for the relevant school or route. Parent accounts must be created by a parent or legal guardian — not by the student themselves.

03 Account linking

Parents link to a driver using a unique driver code or QR code shared directly by that driver. VanLink does not independently verify the real-world identity of drivers or parents beyond the information provided during setup. You're responsible for sharing your driver code only with parents you intend to connect with, and for confirming you're linking to the correct, authorized driver before sharing your child's information.

04 Information we collect

To provide the App's features, we collect: account details you provide (name, phone number, email, photo); driver details (license number, vehicle number, school name, school emergency contact); student details entered by parents and confirmed by drivers (name, class, pickup/drop information); messages sent through in-app chat; and a device push-notification token used to deliver alerts. VanLink does not currently collect continuous GPS location — arrival status is sent manually by the driver. Full detail on how this data is handled is in our Privacy Policy.

05 Third-party services

VanLink is built on Supabase (authentication, database, storage, and real-time messaging) and uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) to deliver push notifications. These providers process data solely to deliver the App's functionality and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

06 How we use your information

Information you provide is used to operate core features: linking drivers and parents, displaying student and route details, enabling chat between a linked driver and parent, and sending arrival-related alerts. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Limited data is cached on your device to allow offline access to your student list and recent messages.

07 Chat & communication

Messages sent through the App are visible only to the driver and parent within a given conversation. Quick-reply and free-text messages are stored to provide message history and offline access. Please don't share sensitive personal information — home addresses, financial details, or government ID numbers — in chat messages.

08 Emergency calling feature

The Emergency screen provides one-tap access to your device's phone dialer for ambulance, police, and school emergency contacts. VanLink does not place calls on your behalf, monitor emergency calls, or guarantee call connectivity. This feature is a convenience tool, not a substitute for direct emergency communication in a crisis. Always confirm you can reach emergency services through your local emergency number if the App is unavailable.

09 Driver responsibilities

Drivers are responsible for the accuracy of pickup/drop details they enter, for confirming the correct student when accepting a pending link from a parent, and for using the App safely — for example, not operating the App while driving. Quick-reply messages are provided to minimize typing while in transit.

10 Parent responsibilities

Parents are responsible for verifying they're linking to the correct, authorized driver for their child's route, and for the accuracy of student details provided during setup. VanLink is a communication and coordination tool; it does not replace direct communication with your child's school regarding transport safety policies.

11 Account suspension & termination

We may suspend or remove access for accounts that misuse the platform — including harassment through chat, providing false driver or student information, or attempting to access another user's account without authorization. You may stop using the App and request account deletion at any time by contacting us.

12 Data retention & deletion

Chat messages, student records, and profile details are retained while your account remains active and linked. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. Some information may be retained where required by law or for legitimate safety-related record-keeping.

13 Security

We apply reasonable technical safeguards, including row-level access controls, to protect your data. No app or service can guarantee absolute security. If a security incident affects your personal information, we will notify affected users and take reasonable steps to address it.

14 Limitation of liability

The App is provided "as is." VanLink is not liable for delays, missed messages, or service interruptions caused by network connectivity, device issues, or third-party service outages. VanLink is a coordination and communication aid and does not guarantee real-time accuracy of pickup, drop-off, or arrival status beyond what is manually reported by the driver.
